## AI-Powered Conflict Avoidance

The central conflict point for couples is not just money, but the **stress of planning and communicating** about it. Amorly solves this by leveraging AI to eliminate the need for negotiation.

### 1. The Core AI Mechanism

We introduce the **AI Date Planning Feature** to transform the app from a budgeting tool into a **Personalized Relationship Concierge**.

| Feature | Description | Value Proposition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emotion-Centric Input** | Users set monthly spending limits and the **Emotional Mood** mix (e.g., $60\%$ Comfort, $30\%$ Stretch). Input focuses on feelings, not logistics, preventing arguments. | **Eliminates Pre-Date Conflict.** |
| **Automated Budget Allocation** | The AI studies historical **(Amount vs. Comfort/Pressure)** data to assign the *right emotional weight* to the available budget for each date. | **Learns the Couple's Financial DNA.** |
| **One-Tap Plan Proposal** | AI generates a concrete, personalized date plan (time, location, estimated cost) that is validated against the couple's comfort zone. | **Conversation-Free Planning:** Couples confirm with a single tap, removing the need for negotiation. |
| **Pressure Filter UX** | The system avoids proposing plans that have a high probability of causing a **'Pressure'** feeling, based on past data and current financial targets. | **Proactively Prevents Financial Strain.** |
