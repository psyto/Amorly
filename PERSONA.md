## 2. üé® Creative Direction

### 2.1. Core Experience
*   **The Vibe**: The app should feel like a shared journal or a cozy living room.
*   **Accessibility First**: Text must be high-contrast and easy to read. No dark-on-dark.
*   **Emotional Safety**: Visuals should calm the user, not excite them.

### 2.2. Visual Language
*   **Color Palette**:
    *   **Base**: Cream / Off-White (`#FAFAF9`)
    *   **Text**: Charcoal / Soft Black (`#292524`) - *Never pure black.*
    *   **Accents**: Sage Green (`#84CC16`), Soft Coral (`#FB7185`), Sky Blue (`#38BDF8`).
*   **Typography**:
    *   **Headings**: **Nunito** or **Quicksand** (Rounded Sans) - Friendly and approachable.
    *   **Body**: **Lato** or **Open Sans** - Clean and legible.
*   **Shapes**: Soft rounded corners (Radius 12-16px). No sharp edges.
*   **Lighting**: Soft, diffused shadows (Paper aesthetics). No heavy neon glows.
